Game Overview & Roles
01
▼
🔵 Crewmate — 11 Players
  • ► Game starts — everyone moves ALONE for the first 3 minutes. No pairing up yet.
  • ► After 3 minutes, you may move in groups of max 2.
  • ► First 5 minutes = Safe Phase. No kills allowed. Explore and socialise.
  • ► Game cannot end within the first 10 minutes.
  • ► Complete your 5 tasks — send photo/video proof to GM.
  • ► Report dead bodies using the whistle (3 blows).
  • ► Vote out impostors in meetings. Trust your gut.
  • ► Win by eliminating ALL impostors.
🔴 Impostor — 2 or 3 Players
  • ► You know who your fellow impostors are — keep it secret.
  • ► Eliminate crewmates by tapping them with the knife prop.
  • ► 30-second cooldown after each kill — honour system. Cheating = automatic loss.
  • ► Fake tasks convincingly. Stand at task locations the right amount of time.
  • ► You may coordinate kills — but never obviously move as a group.
  • ► In meetings, act calm. Accuse others. Never be the quietest person.
  • ► Avoid voting out the Jester — it rewards them and wastes your vote.
  • ► Win if time runs out OR impostors equal/outnumber crewmates.
🃏 Jester — 1 Player
  • ► You are NOT a crewmate and NOT an impostor. You are the wildcard.
  • ► Your goal: get voted out during a meeting.
  • ► If voted out → you win +5 bonus points and are immune from the final forfeit.
  • ► If game ends without you being voted out → no bonus, normal scoring.
  • ► Act a little sus — but not too obvious. Make people suspicious without giving yourself away.
  • ► You complete tasks normally as cover.
⭐ Sheriff — 1 Player
  • ► You are a crewmate with one special power — one bullet.
  • ► During free roam (not meetings), tap a player with your red card and say "I am the Sheriff."
  • ► GM privately checks their role.
  • ► If impostor → they die instantly. Sheriff gets +2 bonus points.
  • ► If wrong (crewmate or Jester) → Sheriff dies instead. -1 point penalty.
  • ► One bullet only. Choose wisely. Never reveal you are Sheriff until you shoot.
Game Master — 1 Person
  • ► Uses the GM panel (hidden link at bottom of page) to assign roles, control timer and soundboard.
  • ► Role Picker assigns roles randomly — no duplicates, correct numbers guaranteed.
  • ► Shows each player their role privately on the phone before the round starts.
  • ► Verifies Sheriff shots — checks role privately, announces result.
  • ► When Jester is voted out, announces dramatically before revealing they are Jester.
  • ► Triggers Force Movement and other events as needed.
  • ► Awards assistant bonus points at own discretion.
⚡ Round Rotation (15 play, 4 assist) 19 players total. 15 play each round. After each round, bottom 4 scorers rotate out and become assistants. Previous assistants rotate back in as players. Tie-breaker: Rock Paper Scissors. Points accumulate across all 4 rounds.
🎭 Role Breakdown Per Round Rounds 1 & 2 → 2 Impostors + 1 Jester + 1 Sheriff + 11 Crewmates (15 total)
Rounds 3 & 4 → 3 Impostors + 1 Jester + 1 Sheriff + 10 Crewmates (15 total)

Step-by-Step Game Flow
03
▼
Pre-Game Setup (10 min before)
P1

GM Prepares Role Cards

Prepare 10 CREWMATE slips and 2 IMPOSTOR slips. Fold and give privately.

P2

GM Tells Impostors Each Other

Tell both impostors who their partner is. Act normal and separate in game.

P3

GM Distributes Task Cards

Give 5 task cards per player. 3 physical, 2 social/mobile/photo. Impostors get fake cards.

P4

Brief Assistants

Brief the 7 assistants: split groups, monitor tasks, record moments, report to GM.

P5

Start!

Blow whistle. Start 35-minute timer. Everyone leaves Living Room. First 5 min = safe phase.

During the Game — The Loop
1

Move to a Zone

Go to any zone in pairs. Keep rotating, no camping.

2

Do a Task & Send Proof

Complete task, take photo/video, send to GM WhatsApp. Build trust or create suspicion.

3

[IMPOSTORS] Look for Opportunities & Kill

Find timing, tap kill, victim drops silent. 30-second cooldown before next kill.

4

Body Found → Whistle → Meeting

Blow whistle 3x. Everyone walks to Living Room eyes half-closed. Keep discussion short.

5

Vote & Resume

Show of hands on GM count. Majority out, tie no out. Resume immediately.

6

Repeat Until Win/Loss

End when both impostors out, impostors match crew, or timer ends.

Tips, Anti-Bore Fixes & Replayability
07
▼
⚡
Keep Meetings Short & Sharp

Hard cap at 4 minutes. If discussion loops, vote and move.

👻
Dead Players Are Content

Give dead players jobs: record clips, help GM timer, observe quietly.

🎯
Impostors: Act Normal, Talk Often

Stay social, do fake tasks, speak in meetings. Silence looks suspicious.

🎲
Prevent Stagnation Early

If no kill by minute 12, trigger Force Movement to shake things up.

🔄
Replayability — Round Tweaks

Try 3 impostors, or add new task cards in later rounds to keep it fresh.

📱
Film & Photograph Everything

Both crewmates and impostors must record or take photo of tasks and send to GM for verification and points. Assistants help record key moments too.

⚠️
Common Game-Breakers to Avoid

Split groups 3+ immediately. No idle camping, no long meetings, no dead-to-alive comms.

🚨 Golden Rule — Simplify ImmediatelyIf game slows down: simplify rules, force movement, keep pace high.
